Who We Are

Built from scratch,
built for the planet.

Rinova is a Greenfield venture born from a single belief: that plastic waste is not a problem to be hidden, but a resource waiting to be reborn. We collect, clean, and chemically transform discarded plastics into high-quality bioplastic resin — then reshape that material into products people love.

Our circular design process means every product begins and ends with purpose. No virgin plastic. No waste sent to landfill. Just design that closes the loop.

Founded by a passionate team from St. Xavier's College, Ahmedabad, Rinova is a Greenfield startup targeting the ₹25,000+ crore Indian eco-materials market.
